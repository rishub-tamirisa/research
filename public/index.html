Rishub Tamirisa

I’m currently working on research toward improving both AI capabilities and alignment.

I recently graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My prior research has focused on developing adversarially robust safeguards for LLMs, while ensuring benign capabilities can improve safely and reliably. My work has been accepted at ICLR, CVPR, NeurIPS, and ICML; also featured in Wired.

Previously, I was a founding engineer at Mindy Group, a Sequoia-backed AI startup. I also co-led Lapis Labs, a student-led research group, and worked at NASA.
