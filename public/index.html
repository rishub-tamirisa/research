Rishub Tamirisa

I am a 4th-year undergraduate at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My research interests are in AI alignment / (mechanistic) interpretability, multimodal representation learning, and privacy-preserving machine learning. I am advised by Dr. Heng Ji.

My current research focuses on developing robust knowledge editing/removal techniques in transformer LLMs, while ensuring benign/helpful capabilities can improve safely and reliably. I have also introduced novel state-of-the-art methods for personalized federated learning. My work has been accepted at ICML, CVPR, and ICLR.

I co-run a research organization called Lapis Labs (prev. AI@UIUC), and am also a founding engineer at a Sequoia-backed AI startup, Mindy Group .
